Chapter 1

A long time ago in a land far, far away the young Queen who ruled the land fell ill.

Well, you know how this goes. The Queen falls ill, the counselors bicker about succession while the nobels orchestrate power grabs, and only the common folk worry about their Queen and pray for her returned good health.

The Queen, though still young, had already endeared herself to the common folk. It was in the hearts and good will of her people that she had the most support, the most love. This was the source of her power.
